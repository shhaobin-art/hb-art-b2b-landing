FAQ

Wholesale Markers — Common Questions

What is the MOQ for wholesale art markers?+

MOQ is flexible and depends on set size and packaging. Stock items can ship in small trial quantities, while OEM/private-label orders typically start from a few thousand sets. Contact us for current minimums and tiered pricing.

Do you offer OEM and private-label markers?+

Yes. We provide full OEM/ODM services including custom ink colors, barrel printing, logo, custom case or display packaging, and private-label branding. Your brand, our factory.

Are your markers certified safe for the USA & EU?+

Yes. Our markers are EN71-3, ASTM D-4236 and REACH compliant, produced in a BSCI-audited factory, making them ready for retail in the USA, EU and UK.

What's the difference between alcohol-based and water-based markers?+

Alcohol-based markers blend smoothly, dry fast and are favored by designers. Water-based markers are low-odor, easy to clean and ideal for kids and classrooms. We manufacture both, including dual-tip and refillable options.

What is the lead time for a markers order?+

Stock items dispatch quickly. Custom OEM orders typically take 25–35 days after artwork and sample approval, depending on quantity and packaging complexity.

Can I get a sample before placing a bulk order?+

Absolutely. We provide samples and a full product catalog for serious B2B buyers. Submit an inquiry with your requirements and our team will arrange sample details within 12 hours.
